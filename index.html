About Trivedi Hospital

Trivedi Hospital, located in Patahi, is dedicated to providing high-quality healthcare services. Our mission is to ensure affordable and reliable medical treatment for every patient.

We specialize in general medicine, emergency care, maternity services, child healthcare, and routine health checkups.
